Eba

Description

This is a quick overview of how to make Eba, a popular Nigerian food. You only need boiling hot water, a sturdy spoon or wooden spatula and garri (See ingredients below) to make eba. For every 1 cup of boiling water used, you will need between ⅓ cup of garri to ½ cup.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Garri

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil on a Stovetop
  2. Add and Stir ⅓ cup of garri to the hot water with a wooden spatula.
  3. If the eba is still soft, sprinkle more garri until desired texture is achieved
  4. Serve it immediately with any Nigerian soup of choice
